Top 3 Plants For Your Living Room.

What if I told you that plants will not only give your space a colorfull desing but can also help you...

  • purify the air
  • control humidity
  • reduce toxins from the air

...and so many other things.

Here I present you 3 plant options to put in you living room, in case you have no idea where to start.

1. Fiddle Leaf Fig

This is an exotic tree that brings a sense of the jungle to your living room

Place it in bright light, but direct summer sun will burn the leaves

2. Snake Plant

This low-maintance favorite has a strong, architectural look that will allow it to stand up in any room.

It is also a great air purifier.

3.Split-leaf Philodendron

This Seventies favorite is back in fashion and is a great statement plant.

Give it a bright or lightly shaded spot and planty of space, it can grow quite large.
